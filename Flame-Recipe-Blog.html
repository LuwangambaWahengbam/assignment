Spicy Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken
  • 2 onions
  • 2 tomatoes
  • Spices
  • Oil & Salt

Preparation

  1. Chop the onions and tomatoes.
  2. Fry onions, add spices and chicken.
  3. Add tomatoes, cook till tender.
  4. Serve hot with rice.

  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce
